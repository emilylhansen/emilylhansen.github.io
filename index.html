Why Software Engineering?

I am a web developer based in New York City, with strong experience developing web applications that utilize Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React, and Redux. I was previously pursuing a double major in computer science and art history at Boston University, also with a double minor in dance and visual arts. I grew up influenced by various forms of art, and I was attracted to cs for its creative and design components. While I enjoyed exploring a wide range of subjects at BU, I felt limited in what I could create in the cs major. I decided to withdraw from college and to explore a career in web development, where I knew I could gain work experience and focus on real-world applications. I’ve found web development to be the perfect marriage between technology and art, my two passions, and I’m glad that I’ve found a field where I can do what I love.
